Tips for best results
  • Name every element. List all components, labels, and connections explicitly. Vague prompts produce vague diagrams.
  • Describe structure, not style. Focus on what elements exist and how they connect. Colors, fonts, and branding are applied automatically.
  • Specify relationships. State flow direction (left-to-right, top-down), groupings, and hierarchy between elements.
  • More detail = better output. Include element counts, exact labels, and spatial arrangement. Longer, specific prompts consistently outperform short ones.
  • Skip the title. Titles belong on slides, not inside the diagram. The system omits them by design.
